Quick Charts:

Automatically generate embeddable, live data charts, graphs and key figures from your spreadsheet

How it works

Quick Charts is powered by HXL tags - a simple standard for interoperable data. Quick Charts scans HXL tags in your spreadsheet to generate bar, pie line charts, and key figures. Supported tags include:

  • #country
  • #adm1
  • #adm2
  • #adm3
  • #adm4
  • #adm5
  • #affected
  • #inneed
  • #reached
  • #targeted
  • #population
  • #date
